Overview

This is a review of a subculture blog that features; Punk Rock, Gothic, Steampunk, Streetwear, Emo & Scene, Cyberpunk, Pin-Up & Rockabilly, Rock & Heavy Metal, Tattooed, Grunge, Vintage & Retro. Harajuku, Boho, Burlesque, Lolita, Kawaii, Pastel Goth, Emo & Scene, and Victorian.

Meeting with stakeholders:

Would like a unique blog website that can:
  • Show featured articles on Homepage
  • Article page
  • Show all category sections
  • Allow readers to sign-up to a newsletter
  • Showcase our Instagram feed
  • Ability to search through content
  • Guest authors
  • Social sharing and liking of posts
  • Article ratings
Pain points
  • Searching for inspiration related to the category sections
  • Get bored with uninspiring content
  • Hate having to return to websites to check for new articles
Deliverables
  • Research references provided and find features that should be added
  • Sketches of initial ideas
  • Journey map
  • Wireframes
  • Homepage
  • Category page
  • Menu
  • Highlight 3 enhancements that have been included to make our blog stand out and solve pain points

Discover

Research Methods
    User interview

      In order to build a better understanding of how the blog is currently used and whether what is required by the client is indeed something the customers will use, I conducted five one-to-one user interviews.

        Issues to address:
        • Does not list all of the topics in dropdown.
        • Ads are distracting.
        • Styles in drop down menu goes to marketplace, not to articles.
        • Would like to be able to login and save articles.

    Competitive testing
    Activities
    • Observe people interacting with the website
    • Have user explore alternative website
    • Discuss likes/dislikes
